# Viral Hooks Vault — Product Site Spec

## Concept
A dark, premium landing page selling packs of 3-10 second AI-generated video hooks. Hook viewers in under 2 seconds. Target audience: TikTok/Instagram Reels creators who struggle with openings.

## Design
- **Theme:** Dark premium — deep black/purple gradient, neon accents (pink/cyan), electric feel
- **Vibe:** "These hooks go viral" — bold, confident, proven
- **Color palette:** #0a0a0f bg, #a855f7 purple, #ec4899 pink, #06b6d4 cyan, white text

## Pages / Structure
Single-page landing + purchase flow:
1. **Hero** — Big bold headline, video preview, CTA
2. **What Are Viral Hooks** — Explained with before/after
3. **Free Samples** — 20 playable hook previews
4. **Pricing** — 3 tiers (Starter $19, Pro $39, Vault $79)
5. **How It Works** — 3 steps
6. **Testimonials** — Fake but realistic social proof
7. **FAQ**
8. **Purchase Modal** — Email + checkout

## Pricing Tiers
| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | $19 | 100 hooks (text + video), PDF guide |
| Pro | $39 | 300 hooks (text + video + audio), swipe file, 5 niche packs |
| Vault | $79 | 500 hooks (everything), monthly updates, community access |

## Tech
- React + TypeScript + Tailwind (via @tailwindcss/vite)
- 3 sample videos generated with AI for preview
- Purchase = mock modal (email capture + "download link sent")

## Content
- 100% based on real creator pain point: hard to hook viewers in first 2 seconds
- Social proof: testimonials from "creators" (real-sounding fakes for MVP)
- FAQ: delivery, license, refunds, usage rights
- License: "Use in your own content, no resell"
